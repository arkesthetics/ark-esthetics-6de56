Frequently Asked Questions About General Dentistry

How much does a routine dental cleaning and exam cost at ARK Esthetics in New York City?
At ARK Esthetics, a standard dental cleaning and exam typically ranges from $150 to $250, depending on your specific needs and whether X-rays are required. We accept most major dental insurance plans, which may cover some or all of the cost. Please contact our office with your insurance details for an exact estimate tailored to your situation.
How soon can I get an appointment for a dental emergency at your New York office?
We prioritize dental emergencies and do our best to accommodate same-day appointments whenever possible. If you’re experiencing severe pain, swelling, or have lost a tooth, call us right away at our Manhattan location. Our team will guide you through immediate steps and fit you in as soon as possible, often within a few hours.
Is ARK Esthetics licensed and are your dentists certified to practice in New York State?
Yes, ARK Esthetics and all our dentists are fully licensed by the New York State Education Department. Our practitioners hold DDS or DMD degrees from accredited institutions and regularly complete continuing education to stay current with the latest dental advancements.
What is the process for getting a dental crown at ARK Esthetics, and how long does it take?
Getting a dental crown at ARK Esthetics usually involves two visits. During your first appointment, we’ll prepare the tooth, take digital impressions, and fit you with a temporary crown. The permanent crown is custom-made by a local dental lab and is typically ready in 10–14 days. At your second visit, we’ll fit and cement the permanent crown, ensuring a comfortable and natural-looking result.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental work performed at your New York office?
We stand behind our dental work and offer a one-year warranty on crowns, bridges, and fillings, provided you maintain your regular check-ups and follow our aftercare instructions. If you experience any issues within this period, please contact us so we can assess and address the problem promptly.
