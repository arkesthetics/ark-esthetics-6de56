What is the average price range for treatments like Botox or dermal fillers at your clinic?
At ARK Esthetics, Botox treatments typically range from $250 to $600 depending on the number of areas treated. Dermal fillers usually start at $700 per syringe. During your initial consultation, we provide a personalized quote based on your goals and the amount of product needed.
How long does a typical med aesthetics appointment take, and can I return to work afterward?
Most med aesthetics appointments, such as Botox or filler injections, take about 30 to 45 minutes, including time for consultation and aftercare instructions. There is minimal downtime, so most clients feel comfortable returning to work or daily activities immediately after their visit.
What does the consultation and treatment process look like at ARK Esthetics?
Your experience starts with a thorough consultation where we discuss your aesthetic goals, review your medical history, and answer any questions. We then recommend a customized treatment plan. On the day of treatment, we cleanse the area, apply a topical numbing agent if needed, and carefully administer the injections. You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions before you leave.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care for your med aesthetic treatments?
While individual results can vary, we stand behind the quality of our services. If touch-ups are needed, we’ll discuss your options to ensure you’re satisfied with your outcome.
