Frequently Asked Questions About Orthodontist

How much do orthodontic treatments typically cost at ARK Esthetics in New York, NY?
The cost of orthodontic treatments at ARK Esthetics varies depending on the type of treatment (traditional braces, clear aligners, etc.) and the complexity of your case. In New York City, comprehensive orthodontic treatment usually ranges from $4,500 to $8,500. We offer complimentary consultations where we provide a personalized treatment plan and detailed cost breakdown. Flexible payment plans and assistance with dental insurance are also available.
How long does the average orthodontic treatment take at your office?
Most orthodontic treatments at ARK Esthetics typically take between 12 to 24 months to complete, depending on your specific needs and the chosen method. During your initial consultation, we’ll assess your teeth and bite, then provide an estimated timeline tailored for you. We schedule regular check-ups at our conveniently located New York City office to monitor your progress and make adjustments as needed.
Are your orthodontists licensed and experienced with complex cases?
Yes, all orthodontists at ARK Esthetics are fully licensed in the state of New York and are members of the American Association of Orthodontists. Our team has extensive experience treating both routine and complex orthodontic cases, including adult and teen patients. We stay up-to-date with the latest techniques and technology to ensure you receive the highest standard of care.
What is the process for starting orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics?
The process begins with a comprehensive consultation at our New York office, where we take digital scans and X-rays to assess your bite and alignment. We’ll discuss your treatment goals, explain your options (such as metal braces, ceramic braces, or clear aligners), and answer any questions you have. Once you decide to proceed, we’ll schedule your fitting or aligner delivery and provide instructions for care and follow-up appointments.
Does ARK Esthetics offer any guarantees or follow-up care after orthodontic treatment?
We stand by the quality of our orthodontic work and provide detailed aftercare guidance to help you maintain your results. After your treatment, we fit you with custom retainers and schedule follow-up visits to ensure your teeth remain in their new positions. While no orthodontic result can be guaranteed for life, we are committed to your long-term satisfaction and offer support should you experience any issues after treatment.
